My Biography

Background

I was born on February 18, 1999 in the beautiful country of Mexico. I lived in Mexico up until I was 3 years old. My parents wanted our family to have a better life so they decided that moving to the USA would benefit us all. We've been in Charlotte ever since and I've grown to love the city.

Hobbies

I like to think that I have many hobbies. I love to draw even though I'm not that good at it. I love to watch soccer. That's much different from actually playing the sport. I enjoy playing video games in my free time although I haven't had much of it lately.

Goals

I am currently in the Road2Hire Tech Cohort. Road2Hire is a nonprofit organization that focuses on sales, leadership, and technology. It promotes economic mobility in young adults aged 18-25. I started the program on the 18th of June of 2018. I've been loving it so far and I plan on making the most out of this opportunity. My goal is to find a career in a field that I love. I'm taking my first steps to achieving this goal.
